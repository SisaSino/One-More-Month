About

One More Month is a gentle support system for people working to stay clean from self-harm. Each month is a fresh start. Whether you made it through or need to try again, you are always welcome here.

How It Works

1. Sign Up

Fill out a short form and choose your role: Buddy (sender), Survivor (receiver), or both.

2. Monthly Check-In

At the end of each month, you'll let us know if you'd like encouragement for staying clean or support to try again.

3. Get Matched

Our team pairs Buddies and Survivors while keeping everyone anonymous and safe.

4. Receive Support

Survivors receive a letter or email with encouragement. Buddies send one thoughtful message each month.

Choose Your Role

Survivor

Receive monthly encouragement and support. You don't need to be "perfect"—every month is a fresh start.

Buddy

Send one letter or message per month offering kindness and encouragement. You're not expected to give advice or therapy.

Both

You can give and receive support at the same time.

If You're Struggling

Relapse does not exclude you from this project.

You are always welcome here. Every month is a chance to begin again.

Our Values

  • Gentle and kind
  • Non-judgmental
  • Anonymous and safe
  • Hopeful, without pressure

Privacy & Safety

  • You can stay completely anonymous
  • Buddies never see your personal address
  • No contact outside the project
  • This is not a therapy or crisis service

Your Safety Matters

This is not a crisis service or a replacement for therapy.

If you are in immediate danger or crisis, please seek professional help.
